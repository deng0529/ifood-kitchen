Why Manshan Kitchen

We are a private kitchen, not a restaurant or takeaway.

We focus on food that is freshly prepared and thoughtfully made — no pre-made dishes, no mass production.

By welcoming only one table of guests each day, we have the time to source fresh ingredients and cook with genuine care.

Shanxi-style noodles and Northern Chinese flavours are rarely found locally. Manshan Kitchen was created to share these familiar tastes, guided by a simple love of cooking and a desire to share it with others.

What you can expect

Handmade noodles and comforting home dishes
Clean, consistent flavours — “home style”
Dine-in only
