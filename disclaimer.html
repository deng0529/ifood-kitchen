Private kitchen

Manshan Kitchen is a private kitchen and is not a restaurant or takeaway business. Dining is available by pre-arrangement only.

Menus and preparation

All meals are freshly prepared in small batches and served as a set menu. Menus may vary depending on seasonal ingredients and availability.

Food allergies

Please inform us in advance of any food allergies or dietary requirements. While we take care in food preparation, we cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.

Bookings and pricing

Menus, pricing and dining arrangements are discussed and confirmed at the time of booking.

Food hygiene

Manshan Kitchen operates from a home kitchen and holds a Food Hygiene Rating of 5 (Highest Rating).
